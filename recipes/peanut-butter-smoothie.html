Peanut butter smoothie

Description

This peanut butter banana smoothie is so refreshing, sweet and tasty!

Ingredients

  • 2 banananas
  • 2 cups of milk
  • 1/2 cup peanut butter
  • 2 tablespoons of honey
  • 2 cups ice cubes

Steps

  1. Place ingredients into blender
  2. Blend for 30 seconds
  3. Serve
